The menu is dominated by dishes of Slavic and Caucasian cuisines, many items from the menu are prepared according to homemade recipes in the author's performance by chefs from Georgia, Ukraine and Belarus.

Here, fresh meat is cooked on an open fire, lard and homemade sausage is served exclusively home-made. Dumplings, ravioli, khinkali are made by hand, and branded khachapuri and shoti bread are baked in a special “Purne” oven on the open part of the kitchen in the presence of guests. Most dishes are prepared immediately after making the order. A varied menu will give the guests of the restaurant a rich palette of tastes.

The bar list includes wines from different parts of the world, a large selection of Georgian wines.
