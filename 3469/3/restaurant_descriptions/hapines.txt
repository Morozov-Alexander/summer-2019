The cafe team "Happiness" welcomes you!
A small cozy cafe with an almost homely atmosphere is located in the science city near Moscow Dubna.
Cafe "Happiness" - a few months of fruitful work of cooks, designers, architects and builders, bartenders and photographers.

Today we are happy to tell you about the results of our work and hope that you will be able to appreciate them.
The interior is designed in Provence style. For painting the walls were used paint golden and purple hue.
On the walls are also located decorative panels of wood of different varieties.
Wooden furniture with antiquities and other interior details are custom-made by professional craftsmen.
